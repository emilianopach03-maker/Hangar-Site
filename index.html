Supporting your technical team with excellent connectivity, nearby housing, and robust local amenities surrounding the AIFA campus.

Nearby Residential Areas

Access to diverse housing options for technicians and staff in growing municipalities like Zumpango, Tecámac, and Tizayuca, offering a range of family-friendly communities and services.

Highways & Main Roads

The site is strategically connected via major highways, including the Circuito Exterior Mexiquense (MEX-M40D) and the Mexico-Pachuca Federal Highway (MEX-85), ensuring efficient commutes.

Airport & Public Access

Multiple access points to the AIFA campus for employees. The AIFA Tren Suburbano station and dedicated bus routes (Mexibús) provide excellent, direct public transport links to and from Mexico City.

Local Services & Amenities

Growing commercial zones provide essential services for daily life:

  • Shopping & Dining: Town Center Zumpango, Plaza Tecámac.
  • Healthcare: IMSS Hospital General de Zona 200, private clinics.
  • Recreation: Parks, sports facilities, and local entertainment.
